What we do

The main activities of Love the Children Inc. are to ensure inclusive well being of the children through financial and Non-Financial support for their Education, Primary Health Care, Foods, and Cloths, Shelter to make a compassionate and generous generation for the world.

How we do

LTC performs it works in two way. First is to raising and collection of fund for the children from the people, individual, donor, events, fundraising programme, Wills, Gifts, Legacy of Love, and Grants.

Our Site

Foods:

The main goal of the programme is to ensure food and recess for every children during school hour so that they feel comfort for their education.

After School Education /Coaching:

The main goal of the programme is to make children handy for their regular activities and assignment to survive with mainstream one.

Emergency Programme:

The main goal of the programme is to ensure adequate support to the children and community to survive from the any calamities to save their lives.

Social/Cultural/Religious Programme:

The main goals of the programme is to ensure social, cultural and religion practice among the children to make a non-fundamental community.

Education and Cloths:

The main goals of the programme is to continue the education and make them equipped to learn with the mainstream children so that all children get an equal justice to their study.

Primary Healthcare Services:

The main goal of the programme is to ensure a safe and good health of each and every children as they have the right to a good health for a good nation.

Home of Love:

The goal of the programme is to ensure a long term and ample services for the distress, orphan and ultra-poor children to ensure their accommodation, food, education, healthcare to build a better generation for the world.
