Our Outcome

  1. Disadvantaged and vulnerable children of the communities can continue their education and get ample support for primary healthcare and other regular needs.
  2. Children are equipped with religion, social, ethical, moral, sports-cultural and extra curriculum knowledge and showing their skills at different levels.
  3. Ensure Emergency support to the disadvantaged and vulnerable children and their families during the disaster and any other calamities or tragedies to survive and reduce life risks.

Our Core Values

  1. We follow the Love of Jesus for Children.'Let the children come to me, and do not hinder them.'
  2. We are committed and dedicated for serving to the poor, marginalized and helpless children's of the world.
  3. We honor of our donor, people, partner and their participation
  4. We are transparent, trustworthy and stewards.
  5. We count Every Children because we love.
